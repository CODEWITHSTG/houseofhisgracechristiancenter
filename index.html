About Us

  • Raising Holy, Healthy and Happy People.

    At House of His Grace Christian Center, our mission is to empower individuals to become holy, healthy, and happy disciples of Jesus Christ. We strive to create a nurturing environment where people from all walks of life can: - Grow in their faith and spiritual maturity (Holy) - Cultivate physical, emotional, and mental well-being (Healthy) - Experience joy, peace, and fulfillment in Christ (Happy)

Faith Building Programs

  • Prayer

    Prayer reaches to the heart of God, and from that secret place where God and the praying soul meet, a mighty force goes out to change the face of things. Prayer is the highest part of life, and it is the secret of all power and strength. It is the key that unlocks the treasures of heaven, and it brings the praying soul into communion with God. .

  • offerings

    Offering is the fundamental characteristic of Christianity. Jesus Christ did not come to earth to acquire but to impart; not to gain but to give; not to receive but to offer. And He calls us to follow Him in this. To offer does not mean simply to give up or surrender something, but to actively present it to God. It means to take our whole life, our every thought, our every possession, and our every capacity, and deliberately offer it to Him.

  • Blessings

    Blessings are always available, but they may come in unexpected packages. Sometimes they're wrapped in struggles, disguised as challenges, or hidden within difficult people. We can choose to see the beauty, the lesson, or the gift in each experience. Or we can focus on the pain, the fear, or the frustration. .
